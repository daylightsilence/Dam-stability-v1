How it works
1. Upload a photo or screenshot of a dam or wall cross-section.
2. Click "Read with AI vision" — the image is sent to Claude, which reads the section shape, dimensions, elevations, soil parameters, and drain info.
3. Extracted values appear below. Click "Apply to input fields" to pre-fill the Input tab — then correct anything misread.
4. Add your Anthropic API key in Settings to enable the vision button. Without a key the app still works fully — just fill Input manually.
